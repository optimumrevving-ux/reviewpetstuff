About ReviewPetStuff

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

We are a small, independent team of pet parents dedicated to providing honest, no-fluff reviews of dog food, raw diets, and cat products.

Our Mission

We believe pet owners deserve transparent information in an industry full of marketing hype. That’s why we test products ourselves, analyze ingredients rigorously, and share real-world results — without sponsored bias.

Why We Focus on Raw & Fresh Feeding

Raw diets and high-quality fresh foods have transformed the health of our own dogs. We want to help more pet parents understand the benefits and make informed choices.

  • ✔ Clear affiliate disclosures
  • ✔ Regular updates as new products and research emerge
