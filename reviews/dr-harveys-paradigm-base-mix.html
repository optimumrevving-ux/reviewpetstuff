Real-World Testing Notes

We tested Dr. Harvey's Paradigm over 8 weeks with a 6-year-old Labrador (prone to weight gain) and a 3-year-old Border Collie. Mixed 1 scoop base with 6–8 oz ground turkey or beef + fish oil and a small amount of organ meat daily.

Both dogs showed noticeable improvements: shinier coat within 3 weeks, excellent stool quality (consistently firm, no odor), steady energy levels, and no digestive upset even during activity changes. The Lab maintained ideal weight easily on a controlled portion.

How to Use This Base Mix

  1. Add 1 scoop Paradigm per 10–15 lbs body weight daily
  2. Mix with fresh ground meat (80/10/10 ratio recommended)
  3. Add fish oil or other healthy fats
  4. Rehydrate slightly with warm water or bone broth for better acceptance
  5. Transition gradually over 7–10 days
