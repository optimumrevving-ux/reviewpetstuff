Our 7-Factor Ranking Methodology

Every product is scored on a 1–10 scale across these weighted categories. The final score determines its position on our hub pages.

1. Ingredient Quality — 25%

Named meats first? Human-grade? Minimal fillers, by-products, or artificial additives?

2. Nutritional Balance — 20%

AAFCO standards, proper macronutrient ratios, and micronutrient completeness.

3. Digestibility & Palatability — 15%

Stool quality, vomiting reports, and acceptance by picky eaters.

4. Real User Feedback — 15%

Volume and sentiment from Amazon, Chewy, and raw feeding communities.

5. Value for Money — 10%

Cost per serving and portion efficiency.

6. Brand Transparency — 10%

Recall history, third-party testing, and sourcing clarity.

7. Special Needs Fit — 5%

Performance for allergies, seniors, active dogs, etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept free products for review?

No. We purchase all products ourselves or use real customer data to maintain complete independence.

How often are rankings updated?

At least once per month, or more frequently when new data (reviews, recalls, formula changes) becomes available.

Can a brand pay to be ranked higher?

Never. Sponsored content is clearly labeled when it appears, and it never affects rankings.

Why do your rankings sometimes differ from other sites?

We put heavy emphasis on ingredient quality and real-world digestibility rather than marketing claims or affiliate revenue.
